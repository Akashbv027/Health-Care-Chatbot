General User Manual
  1. Before Taking: Read all instructions and warnings. Check expiry date. Inform doctor of other medications.
  2. Storage: Keep in original container at room temperature (15-25°C). Away from moisture and sunlight.
  3. Timing: Set phone reminders for regular doses. Do not skip or double dose.
  4. Food & Drink: Some medications require food, others on empty stomach. Check instructions. Avoid alcohol.
  5. Side Effects: Common: nausea, dizziness, drowsiness. Contact doctor if severe or persistent.
  6. Interactions: Inform doctor of all medications, supplements, and herbal products you take.
  7. Pregnancy/Nursing: Always consult doctor if pregnant or breastfeeding before taking any medication.
  8. Emergencies: If overdose or severe reaction occurs, call emergency services or poison control immediately.
  9. Follow-up: See doctor as scheduled even if symptoms improve. Keep all appointments.
  10. Disposal: Do not flush medications. Take to pharmacy or designated disposal site.
