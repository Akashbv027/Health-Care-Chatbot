====================================================================
REPORTED / COMMON SYMPTOMS SUPPORTED
====================================================================

Fever, cough (dry/wet), cold, sore throat, headache, body pain,
muscle pain, weakness, dizziness, breathlessness, chest pain,
stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, skin rash, swelling,
urinary pain, mental stress, fatigue.

====================================================================
SYMPTOM-BASED MEDICINE & CARE GUIDANCE
====================================================================

--------------------------------------------------
1) FEVER / BODY PAIN / HEADACHE
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Viral infection, dehydration, seasonal illness

Medicine:
- Paracetamol 500–650 mg
Dosage:
- 1 tablet every 6–8 hours (ONLY if fever/pain present)
Maximum:
- Do NOT exceed 3000 mg in 24 hours
Duration:
- 2 to 3 days only

Home care:
- Rest well
- Drink warm water
- Lukewarm sponging for high fever

Consult doctor if:
- Fever lasts more than 3 days
- Fever returns repeatedly
- Fever is above 103°F

--------------------------------------------------
2) COUGH / SORE THROAT / COLD
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Viral upper respiratory infection

Medicine:
- Dry cough → Dextromethorphan syrup
- Wet cough → Guaifenesin expectorant

Dosage:
- 5–10 ml, 2 to 3 times daily
Duration:
- 3 to 5 days

Sore throat care:
- Warm saltwater gargling (2–3 times/day)
- Warm fluids, avoid cold drinks

Consult doctor if:
- Cough lasts more than 7 days
- Blood in cough
- Severe breathlessness

--------------------------------------------------
3) BREATHLESSNESS / CHEST TIGHTNESS (MILD)
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Anxiety, infection, asthma history

Immediate steps:
- Sit upright
- Slow deep breathing
- Avoid exertion

IMPORTANT:
- NO self-medication

EMERGENCY if:
- Breathlessness at rest
- Blue lips/fingertips
- Chest pain with sweating

--------------------------------------------------
4) CHEST PAIN (CRITICAL SYMPTOM)
--------------------------------------------------
Possible serious causes:
- Heart attack
- Blood clot
- Severe infection

WARNING SIGNS (CALL EMERGENCY IMMEDIATELY):
- Crushing or pressure-like chest pain
- Pain spreading to left arm/jaw/back
- Sweating, nausea, dizziness
- Sudden breathlessness

ACTION:
- Go to nearest emergency hospital immediately

--------------------------------------------------
5) STOMACH PAIN / VOMITING / DIARRHEA
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Food infection, viral gastroenteritis

Care:
- ORS solution after loose stools
- Light food (rice, curd, banana)
- Avoid oily/spicy food

Medicine:
- ORS only (NO antibiotics without doctor)

Consult doctor if:
- Vomiting lasts >24 hours
- Blood in vomit or stool
- Severe abdominal pain

--------------------------------------------------
6) DIZZINESS / WEAKNESS
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Low BP, dehydration, stress

Care:
- Sit or lie down
- Drink fluids
- Avoid sudden standing

Consult doctor if:
- Repeated fainting
- One-sided weakness
- Speech difficulty

--------------------------------------------------
7) SKIN RASH / SWELLING
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Allergy, infection

Care:
- Keep area clean and dry
- Avoid scratching

Medicine:
- Antihistamine ONLY if advised

EMERGENCY if:
- Facial or throat swelling
- Difficulty breathing
- Rapidly spreading rash

--------------------------------------------------
8) URINARY PAIN / BURNING
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Urinary infection or dehydration

Care:
- Drink plenty of water
- Do NOT hold urine

Consult doctor if:
- Blood in urine
- Fever with urinary pain
- Symptoms >2 days

--------------------------------------------------
9) MENTAL STRESS / ANXIETY
--------------------------------------------------
Possible cause:
- Overwork, illness stress

Care:
- Proper sleep
- Breathing exercises
- Reduce screen time
- Talk to someone trusted

Consult doctor if:
- Panic attacks
- Depression lasting >2 weeks
- Thoughts of self-harm

--------------------------------------------------
1) MIGRAINE
--------------------------------------------------
Possible symptoms:
- vomiting, body, pain, headache, fever

Home care:
- Rest in dark room, avoid screen

Doctor may prescribe:
- Paracetamol 650mg + Domperidone once during pain

Consult doctor if:
- Symptoms worsen
- Symptoms last more than 3-5 days
- Fever above 103°F or severe pain

2) HEART PROBLEM
--------------------------------------------------
Possible symptoms:
- vomiting, body, pain, headache, fever

Home care:

Doctor may prescribe:
- Immediate hospital visit – ECG and cardiologist consultation required

Consult doctor if:
- Symptoms worsen
- Symptoms last more than 3-5 days
- Fever above 103°F or severe pain

- Symptoms last more than 3-5 days

====================================================================
LONG-DURATION SYMPTOMS RULE (7 DAYS ALERT)
====================================================================

ANY symptom lasting more than 7 days requires medical evaluation:
- Persistent fever
- Continuous cough
- Long-term fatigue
- Chronic pain
- Recurrent swelling

These may indicate:
- Bacterial infection
- Chronic illness
- Hormonal disorder
- Serious disease

====================================================================
SERIOUS RED-FLAG CONDITIONS (DO NOT IGNORE)
====================================================================

BLOOD CLOT WARNING:
- Sudden chest pain
- One leg swelling/pain
- Sudden breathlessness

CANCER WARNING:
- Unexplained weight loss
- Persistent lump or swelling
- Blood in urine, cough, or stool
- Symptoms lasting weeks/months

HEART ATTACK WARNING:
- Severe chest pressure
- Left arm/jaw pain
- Sweating + nausea

STROKE WARNING:
- One-sided weakness
- Slurred speech
- Facial drooping

ACTION:
- Seek emergency medical care immediately
